Why Road Damage AI

Built for real roads, real conditions

🎯

Multi-Model Ensemble

Three YOLOv8s models trained on different datasets and camera perspectives — dashcam, close-up, and street-level — for comprehensive coverage.

🤝

Consensus Detection

When 2 or more models agree on a detection, it's flagged as high-confidence consensus. Multi-model agreement dramatically reduces false positives.

📡

Live Camera Mode

Point your webcam at a road. The system analyzes frames in real-time, tracks cumulative damage count, and computes the damage area percentage.

🔗

Transfer Learning Chain

Each model inherits knowledge from the previous: COCO → RDD2022 → Pothole-600 → Kaggle. Progressive specialization for maximum accuracy.

⚡

Low Confidence Recall

Model A and C run at conf=0.05 to catch early-stage damage. This high-recall approach surfaces defects that a stricter threshold would miss.

📊

Damage Area Analytics

Live mode computes the percentage of the road surface classified as damaged, giving you a quick severity score per frame alongside raw detection counts.

How the ensemble works

Input Image / Frame

02

3-Model Inference

All three YOLOv8 models run independently on the same image at their calibrated confidence thresholds.

→
03

Cross-Model NMS

Overlapping detections across models are merged using Non-Maximum Suppression (IoU=0.45). Agreeing models are tracked.

→
04

Results & Analytics

Color-coded bounding boxes, detection table, consensus badges, and damage area percentage are shown instantly.
